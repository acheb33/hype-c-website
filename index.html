Ingredient Disclosure

California Cleaning Product Right to Know Act of 2017 (SB 258)

In compliance with California's Cleaning Product Right to Know Act, Hype Everything LLC provides the following information about the ingredients in Hype-C Surface Cleaner.

Product

Hype-C Surface Cleaner, 16 FL OZ (473 mL)

Complete Ingredient List

Ingredient Chemical Formula CAS Number Function
Water H₂O 7732-18-5 Solvent / Base
Sodium Chloride NaCl 7647-14-5 Electrolyte
Hypochlorous Acid HOCl 7790-92-3 Active Cleaning Agent

Product Use

Hype-C is a surface cleaner intended for use on hard, non-porous surfaces. Spray on any surface, wipe or let dry. No rinsing required.

Safety

Hype-C contains no intentionally added fragrances, dyes, preservatives, or allergens. The product is non-toxic, biodegradable, and made with a gentle formula. It contains no bleach (sodium hypochlorite), no alcohol, and no volatile organic compounds (VOCs).

Regulatory Status

Hype-C is a surface cleaner. It is not registered with the EPA as a pesticide, disinfectant, or sanitizer, and no claims of antimicrobial efficacy are made.
